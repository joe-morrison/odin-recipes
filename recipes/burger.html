Beef Burger

Description

Make the perfect burger

Ingredients

  • Burger rolls
  • Beef burger patties
  • Cheese (sliced)
  • Tomatoes (sliced)
  • Lettuce
  • Sauce
  • Balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Fry the patties in a pan for approx 7 minutes until cooked
  2. Add lettuce leaves and sliced tomato to the bun
  3. Add the patty to the bun and top with a slice of cheese
  4. Finally, add the sauce and serve
