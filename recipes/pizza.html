Pizza

Description

A delicious homemade pizza

Ingredients

  • A large pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Spread pizza sauce onto the base
  2. Grate mozarella cheese over the pizza base
  3. Add toppings
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with Italian spices
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for 15 minutes
  6. Slice and serve hot!
