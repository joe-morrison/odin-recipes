Pasta

Description

A delicious pasta salad with salami, cheese and veg

Ingredients

  • Pasta screws
  • Salami (diced)
  • Cheese (diced)
  • Cherry tomatoes (halfed)
  • Assorted veg of your choice
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Add the pasta screws to boiling water, add salt and cook for 20 minutes until 'el dente'
  2. Drain the cooked pasta and pour into a large bowl
  3. Add salami, cheese, tomatoes and veg
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  5. Add pepper and seasoning to taste and stir lightly
  6. Serve warm
